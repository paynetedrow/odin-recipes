Hamburgers

Awesome Hamburgers

Hamburgers are simply great. You can eat hamburgers a couple times a month consisently. Let me know how you feel about hamburgers.

Do not over cook those hamburgers. Also do not under cook them. I'd keep it on the grill if it were me.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Veggies
  • Meat
  • Condiments

Steps

  1. Cook meat
  2. Top bun with condiments
  3. Chop Veggies
  4. Build burger
